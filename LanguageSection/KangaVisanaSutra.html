Khanga Visaṇa Sutra

British Library Fragment 5B

sarveṣo bhuteṣo ṇis̱ae daṁḍo.
avihesao aṁñataraṁ pi teṣo
metreṇa citiṇa hitaṇ‍ukaṁpi
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
When you’ve laid down arms toward all creatures,
not harming even a single one,
don’t wish for a child, let alone a companion:
live alone like a horned rhino.
saṁs‍evamaṇas̱a siyati seho
sehaṁvayaṁ dukham idaṁ prabhoti
[Adinavaṁ sehajo pre‍kṣamaṇa]
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Those with close relationships have affection,
following which this pain arises.
Seeing this danger born of affection,
live alone like a horned rhino.
m‍i‍tra s‍ahaya [anukampamaṇo]
haveti artho paḍivadha­cita
[Eto bhayo santhave pre‍kṣamaṇa]
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
When feelings for friends and loved ones
are tied up in selfish love, you miss out on the goal.
Seeing this peril in intimacy,
live alone like a horned rhino.
vaṁśo viśalo va yasa viṣato
putreṣo dareṣo ya ya avekho
vaṁsakaḍiro va aṣajamaṇo
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
As a spreading bamboo gets entangled,
so does concern for partners and children.
Like a bamboo shoot unimpeded,
live alone like a horned rhino.
mrigo abadho vi yas̱a araṁñe
yeṇichati gachati goyarae
[Viṁñu naro seritaṁ pre‍kṣamaṇa]
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
As a wild deer loose in the forest
grazes wherever it wants,
a smart person looking for freedom would
live alone like a horned rhino.
putra ya dara yaṁ dhaṇo jahitva
parigrahe ñati a baṁdhava ya
+ + + + + + + + + + +
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
son and wife even wealth have been abandoned,
possession, relatives and even tribe which,
+ + + + + + + + + + +
live alone like a horned rhino.
saṁkriḍaṇa bhoti sahayamaje
putreṣo ya viul‍a bhoti prema
[Piya vipa yogo vijiguchamano]
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Among friends you have fun and games,
and for children you are full of love.
Though loathe to depart from those you hold dear,
live alone like a horned rhino.
amaṁtraṇa bhoti maha­yaṇas̱a
[Vase ṭhane gamane carikaia
Anabhijitaṁ seritaṁ pre‍kṣamaṇa]
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
When among friends, whether staying in place
or going on a journey, you’re always on call.
Looking for the uncoveted freedom,
live alone like a horned rhino.
dosaṁgraha pravra‍yida vi ege
[Atho gahaṭhp gharamavasanta
Avossuko paraputesu hutva]
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Even some renunciates are hard to please,
as are some layfolk dwelling at home.
Don’t worry about others’ children,
live alone like a horned rhino.
kriḍa [ratiṁ kamasukhanaloge
Analaṅkaritva anavekhamaṇo;
Vibhusanaṭhaṇa virato sacavadi]
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
When you realize that worldly fun and games
and pleasure are unsatisfying, disregarding them,
as one unadorned, a speaker of truth,
live alone like a horned rhino.
aradha­virya [paramathapatiya,
Alinacito akusitavuti;
Darhanikamo thamabalupavanno],
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
With energy roused to reach the ultimate goal,
not sluggish in mind or lazy,
vigorous, strong and powerful,
live alone like a horned rhino.
ras̱e agridhaṁ [akaro alolo,
Anaṁñavosi savadanacari;
Kule kule avaṭibadhacito]
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Not wanton, nor rousing greed for tastes,
providing for no other, wandering indiscriminately for alms,
not attached to this family or that,
live alone like a horned rhino.
pava sahaya parivajaea
aṇartha­sevi viṣame ṇiviṭha
[Sayo na seve pasuto pamatto]
eko care khargaviṣaṇagapo.
Avoid a wicked companion,
blind to the good, habitually immoral.
Don’t befriend the heedless and the hankering, but
live alone like a horned rhino.
aṇuchatio ahiacae to
viṁñu­praśastaṁ ca suho ya dhaṁmo
ṇa putraṁ i‍chea kuto sahayo
[eko] ca‍r‍e kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Reluctantly they asks for thus,
wise and poetic pleasant dharma,
no child would want that friend,
live alone like a horned rhino.
aṭ́ha‍ṇa to saṁgaṇiaradas̱a
yaṁ phaṣae samaïa vimuti
adicabaṁdhus̱a vayo ṇiśama
eko care khargaviṣaṇaga‍po.
It’s impossible for one who delights in company
to experience even temporary freedom.
Heeding the speech of the kinsman of the Sun,
live alone like a horned rhino.
dispa suvarṇas̱a prabha‍svaraṇi
kamara­putreṇa suṇiṭhidaṇi
saṁghaṭamaṇaṇi due bhuyasiṁ
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Though made of shining gold, well-finished by a smith,
when two bracelets share the same arm
they clash up against each other. Seeing this,
live alone like a horned rhino.
kama hi citra mas̱ora maṇorama
vir‍u‍par‍u‍v‍e‍h‍i mas̱eṁti cita
adiṇava kamaguṇeṣo di‍spa
eko care khargaviṣaṇagapo.
Sensual pleasures are diverse, sweet, delightful,
appearing in disguise they disturb the mind.
Seeing danger in sensual stimulations,
live alone like a horned rhino.
putraṁ va mitra maritaṁ va m eva
hitvaṇu kamaṇi yas̱otoaṇi
[dhanani dhaṁñaṇi ya bandhavaṇi]
ek‍o care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Children, partner, father, mother,
wealth and grain and relatives:
having given up sensual pleasures to this extent,
live alone like a horned rhino.
ośaḍaïta gihi­viṁjaṇaṇi
ośiṇapatro yas̱a koviraḍo
kaṣa‍yavastro abhinikhamitva
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Having shed the marks of the home life,
like the fallen leaves of the Shady Orchid Tree,
and gone forth in the ocher robe,
live alone like a horned rhino.
saṁdalaïta gihibaṁdhaṇaṇi
jalaṁ yas̱a bhitva balaṁ śaüto
agiva daḍha aṇivart‍amaṇa
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Having burst apart the marks of the home life,
like a fish that is strong and tears a net,
or a fire not returning to ground it has burned,
live alone like a horned rhino.
sih‍o va chadeṣo asaṁtrasaṁta
vato va jalasiṁ aṣajamaṇa
[padumo va toyena alivamaṇo]
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Like a lion not startled by sounds,
like wind not caught in a net,
like water not sticking to a lotus,
live alone like a horned rhino.
sih‍o [yas̱a daṭhabali pasayha,
Raja migaṇo abhibhuya cari;
Sevetha pantaṇi senasanaṇi],
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Like the fierce-fanged lion, king of beasts,
who wanders as victor and master,
you should frequent remote lodgings, and
live alone like a horned rhino.
śikṣa samadae achidra­vurti
kalaṇa­śilaṁ bhayavajadaśi
ichea na putra kuto sahayo
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Training is a battle with out a habit,
beutiful conduct in seeing dreads and faults,
wish not a child from a friend,
live alone like a horned rhino.
bhayea mitra paḍibhaṇavaṁta
baho­ṣuda dhaṁma­dhara uraḍa
aṁñae dhaṁmaṁ viyigitsa prahae
ek‍o care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
An eloquent and uplifting friend,
spend time with the learned who have memorized the teachings,
when you understand the dharma and have gained light,
live alone like a horned rhino.
sayi labhea ṇivago sahayo
sardhacare sas̱ovihari dhiro
abhibhuya sarvaṇi pariṣeaṇi
carea teṇata­maṇa svadima.
If you find an alert companion,
a wise and virtuous friend,
then, overcoming all adversities,
wander with them, joyful and mindful.
ṇo ya labhea ṇivago sahayo
sardhacare sas̱o­vihari dhiro
raya va raṭha viyidaṁ prahae
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gap‍o.
If you find no alert companion,
no wise and virtuous friend,
then, like a king who flees his conquered realm,
wander alone like a tusker in the wilds.
ardha prahiśati sahaya­saṁpata
śreṭh‍a sama se‍v‍itava sahaya
[Eto alada anavajabhoa.]
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Clearly we praise the blessing of a friend,
it’s good to be with friends your equal or better.
but failing to find them, eating blamelessly.
live alone like a horned rhino.
okṣita­cakhu yas̱a­cari gramo
gutiṁdrio rakṣati maṇas̱aṇo
[Anavassuto apariḍayhamaṇo]
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Eyes downcast, not footloose,
senses guarded, mind protected,
uncorrupted, not burning with desire,
live alone like a horned rhino.
salaṇa to jaṇo aricamaṇa
dhaṁmeṣo ṇico aṇudhaṁma­yari
[adinavo samasita bhavesu]
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Not neglecting retreat and absorption,
always living in line with the teachings,
comprehending the danger in rebirths,
live alone like a horned rhino.
driṭhi­viṣoaṇi uvativuto
prata ṇiamo paḍiladha­mago
[Uvannañaṇomhi anaṁñaneyo]
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Thinking, “I have left twisted views behind,
reached the sure way, and gained the path;
I’ve given rise to knowledge, and need no-one to guide me”
live alone like a horned rhino.
śita ca uṣ̄a ca kṣus̱a pivas̱a
[Vatatape ḍaṁsasarisape ca]
[Sarva nipetaṇi abhisambhavïta]
eko care khargaviṣaṇagapo.
Heat and cold, hunger and thirst,
wind and sun, flies and snakes:
having put up with all these things,
live alone like a horned rhino.
ṇago vi yus̱aṇi vivajaïta
saṁjada­kaṁdho patumaṁ uraḍo
yas̱abhiraṁta viha‍r‍e araṁñe
eko care kharga­v‍iṣaṇa­gapo.
As a full-grown elephant, lotus-eating, magnificent,
forsaking the herd,
stays as it pleases in the forest,
live alone like a horned rhino.
ṇiloluo ṇikuho ṇikaṣayo
ṇimoho ṇidhaṁto­kaṣaya­mrakṣo
ṇiraṣayo sarva­lo‍g‍e bhavitva
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
No greed, no guile, no thirst, no slur,
dross and delusion is smelted off;
free of hoping for anything in the world,
live alone like a horned rhino.
catudi‍śo apaḍiho kuhi ca
saṁtuśamaṇa itaridareṇa
pariṣeaṇa sahita achaṁbi
eko care khargaviṣaṇaga‍po.
At ease in any quarter, unresisting,
content with whatever comes your way;
prevailing over adversities, dauntless,
live alone like a horned rhino.
raga ca doṣa ca prahae mokho
taṣ̄a ya sarva­sayoy‍aṇa‍ṇi
asaṁtrasaṁ jivita­saṁ‍śayasiṁ
ek‍o care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Having given up greed, hate, and delusion,
having burst apart the fetters,
unafraid at the end of life,
live alone like a horned rhino.
metra uvekha karuṇa ya bhavae
asevamaṇa mutita e kalo
sarveṇa logeṇa a‍virujama‍ṇ‍a
eko care kharga­vi‍ṣaṇa­gapo.
In time, cultivate freedom through
love, compassion, rejoicing, and equanimity.
Not upset by anything in the world,
live alone like a horned rhino.
prahaï paṁcavaraṇaṇi cedaso
uvakileśa vavaṇuja sar‍v‍a
abhibhuya sarvaṇi pariṣeaṇi
eko care kharga­viṣa‍ṇa­gapo.
When you’ve given up five mental obstacles,
and expelled all corruptions,
and cut off affection and hate, being independent,
live alone like a horned rhino.
kitañata taṇi maṇuśaloge
sudulabho baḍo śaḍha maṇuśa
ṇiraṣayo sar‍va­loge bhavitva
eko care kharga­viṣaṇ‍a­gapo.
Gratefulness into this human world,
very difficult to obtain greatness from a deceitful human,
to have been independent of all the world,
live alone like a horned rhino.
bhayaṁti sevaṁti ya karaṇarthia
ṇigaraṇo dulabha aja mitra
kule kule apa‍ḍivadha­cita
eko care kharga­viṣa‍ṇa­gapo.
They befriend you and serve you for their own sake;
these days it’s hard to find friends lacking ulterior motive,
kinsman of kinsman they fall formless.
live alone like a horned rhino.
evaṁ khu masa dutieṇa sadha
vayabhilavo ahiṣajaṇa va
eto bhaya ayati pre‍kṣamaṇa
eko care kharga­viṣaṇa­gapo.
Thinking, “So too, if I had a partner,
there’d be flattery or curses.”
Seeing this peril in the future,
live alone like a horned rhino.
